🔁 Repeated High-Yield Topics

  • 1. Base Conversions: Binary, Octal, Decimal, Hex (including fractions).
  • 2. Complements: 1's and 2's complement subtraction methodology.
  • 3. De Morgan's Law: Statements and proofs via Truth Tables.
  • 4. Boolean Simplification: Algebraic reduction & Canonical form conversions.
  • 5. Karnaugh Maps: 3-variable and 4-variable maps with Don't Care conditions.

📝 Examiner Preferences

  • Numerical Dominant: ~80% of marks focus on calculation and logic diagrams.
  • Truth Table Proofs: Preferred over complex algebraic identities.
  • Textbook Integrity: Problems are often direct variations of Morris Mano examples.
  • No Derivations: Focus remains on application rather than pure theory.
